About

In the hub of education located at New York University Abu Dhabi is the well-known Blacksmith Coffee Company. Since its opening, Blacksmith has been the go-to hang-out area for students and other individuals to work, study, and socialize with each other. With its delicious roasted and brewed coffee Blacksmith has attracted many customers around the city, ultimately creating a unique and enjoyable atmosphere.

Production of 30MFF

The 30 Minute Film Festival is a competition among filmmakers that aims to support and enhance their filmmaking skills. As part of the Communications Lab course, this competition was given to students on their first day in class. The 30MFF provides students with 30 minutes to produce a 1.5-minute film, where they have 15 minutes of filming and 15 minutes of editing.

As part of the 30MFF, our group decided to produce a short film on Blacksmith NYUAD. The film aims to show NYUAD student's connection to the Blacksmith coffee shop and capture the beautiful ambiance of the place.
